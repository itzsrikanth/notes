# My Nature & Character

*Living document — input for projects, career decisions, and self-reflection*

---

## Core Identity

I am a **self-taught, curiosity-driven engineer** who has always learned by going wide before going deep. I don't wait for permission or a syllabus — I chase what fascinates me, even if the payoff isn't immediate.

## Character Traits

### 🔥 Relentless Curiosity
- I naturally gravitate towards learning things outside my job scope — kernel programming, share market trading, electronics, AI — all explored on my own time and energy, even as a L2 support engineer in a Java-Unix project
- I don't learn to tick boxes; I learn because something *clicks* and I can't stop pulling the thread
- This curiosity is both my superpower and my challenge — without direction, it scatters energy across too many pursuits

### 🔄 Adaptability Over Regret
- I don't dwell on missed paths. When I recognize that scattered learning without mentorship didn't yield fruit, my response is **willingness to adapt, not bitterness**
- I reframe setbacks as context: "Had I been coached by a mentor, I could have touched heights" — but I carry this as **awareness, not regret**
- This adaptability has driven every major career pivot I've made

### 🧭 Pattern Recognition in Industry Shifts
- I don't just follow trends — I sense **when a technology stack is reaching its ceiling** and position myself ahead
- Frontend → Full-stack (when server-driven UI made pure frontend roles scarce)
- Full-stack → AI/LLM Inferencing (staying on the edge, not running behind the tech stack)
- This is a deliberate strategy: **always be where the wave is forming, not where it's breaking**

### 🛡️ Privacy & Ethics Consciousness
- Deep discomfort with surveillance capitalism and closed-source data exploitation
- Motivated to build OSS alternatives not just for personal use but so **everyone** can benefit
- The Meta Ray-Ban "pervert glasses" problem isn't abstract to me — it's a call to action

### 🧱 Builder Mentality
- I don't just theorize — I ship. From frontend UIs to full-stack products to OSS contributions (SGLang)
- I think in systems: how does this tool connect to users, to privacy, to the market?
- I'd rather build something imperfect and iterate than plan forever

### 📚 Self-Directed but Mentor-Aware
- My entire career has been self-taught — L2 support → frontend → full-stack → AI, all without formal guidance
- I now recognize the **value of mentorship** — not as a crutch, but as a force multiplier
- I actively seek structured frameworks and communities to give my curiosity direction

1. **Not being left behind** — I've seen tech stacks die. I refuse to be the person still optimizing jQuery when the world moved on
